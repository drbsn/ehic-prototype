Apply for healthcare cover abroad

There are two types of cover available, use this service to apply for either a:

  • UK Global Health Insurance Card (GHIC)
  • UK European Health Insurance Card (EHIC)

Who can apply for a UK GHIC

You can apply for a UK GHIC if you:

  • live in the UK
  • normally live in the UK but are studying in the EUr
  • live in the EU and have been issued with a UK S1 or A1 document
  • an eligible family member or dependant of one of the above
