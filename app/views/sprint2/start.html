Get healthcare cover for visiting the EU (EHIC)

Use this service to apply for an EHIC. An EHIC gives you the right to access state-provided healthcare on temporary stays in countries in the EU/EEA and Switzerland.

You are eligible if:
  • You are a UK citizen
  • You are an EU citizen with settled or pre-settled status (indefinite leave to reamin in the UK)
