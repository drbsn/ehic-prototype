Welcome to the Overseas Healthcare Services online portal

Thank you for taking part in the pre-registration process for a new European Health Insurance Card (EHIC).

EHIC from 1 January 2021

The UK left the European Union (EU) on 31 January 2020 and is now in a transition period until the end of 31 December 2020.

From 1 January 2021, people living in another EEA country or Switzerland by the end of the transition period who hold a registered, UK-issued S1 certificate will continue to access healthcare in the same way they currently do. As now, those who meet the aforementioned criteria will also be entitled to an EHIC.

The EHIC will cover you for all medically necessary, state-funded, needs-arising healthcare when visiting another EEA country or Switzerland (other than the country you are residing in and the UK).

The pre-registration process

During the S1 pre-registration EHIC process, you will be asked to respond to a series of questions and to enter your relevant details into the Overseas Healthcare Services online portal. By taking part, you will be able to apply for an EHIC as an S1 holder prior to 1 January 2021. This will ensure you have your EHIC ready for the end of the transition period.

If your application is not successful, and you do not meet the eligibility criteria to entitle you to an EHIC as an S1 holder, you will be notified during the pre-registration process. Should your application for an EHIC be successful, you will receive email confirmation at the address you supply in your application.

Please refer to your confirmation email for further information, including when you will receive your new EHIC.
