Get healthcare cover for travelling abroad

Information:
The Global Health Insurance Card (GHIC) replaces the European Health Insurance Card (EHIC) for most UK residents. Some people can still get a European Health Insurance Card.

Use this service to:

  • check if you are eligible
  • apply for either a UK Global Health Insurance Card (GHIC) or UK European Health Insurance Card (EHIC)

Where are the cards valid

Both cards cover you for necessary state provided healthcare treatment whilst temporarily staying in an EU country. An EHIC also covers treatment in Norway, Iceland, Liechtenstein and Switzerland. You cannot use a GHIC in Norway, Iceland, Liechtenstein and Switzerland. More countries may be added to those accept GHIC in the future.
