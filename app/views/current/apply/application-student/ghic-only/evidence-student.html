You may be entitled to a student UK Global Health Insurance Card (GHIC)

Based on the information you have given us, you may be entitled to a student UK GHIC while you study.

You should make sure a UK GHIC covers the country you're studying in. Check our website to see where you can use your card (opens in a new tab), then return here to complete your application.

As part of your application, you'll need to provide evidence of where you're studying.

If your application is successful, your student UK GHIC will only be valid for the duration of your period of study.
