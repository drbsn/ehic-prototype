Beta

October 2020 - June 2021

GDS Beta assessment - 15 June 2021 Criteria not met

Why didn't we pass assessment?

The panel was not completely satisfied that the service team conducted user research with a diverse enough range of participants. Due to the Covid-19 pandemic we were restricted to testing user journeys remotely with people who were comfortable using technology. It was not possible to recruit participants and conduct user testing 'in real life' with users who may have a lower level of digital literacy.
