Which countries accept UK GHIC and UK EHIC

Each card provides the same cover but are valid in different countries. During your application we’ll let you know what card you’re entitled to.

UK Global Health Insurance Card (UK GHIC)

Usually, UK nationals living in the UK will get a UK GHIC. Some other people, like students studying in one of the countries listed below can also get a UK GHIC.
