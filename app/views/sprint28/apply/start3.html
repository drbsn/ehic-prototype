Get healthcare cover for traveling within the EU

Use this service to check your eligibility and apply for either a:

UK Global Health Insurance Card (GHIC)

This covers:

  • state-provided healthcare cover for treatment that becomes necessary whilst temporarily staying in an EU country
  • you if you are a student studying outside of the UK, a UK GHIC will cover you for both your country of study if this is in the EU, as well as any other EU country. You will not be able to use your card for treatment in Norway, Iceland, Liechtenstein and Switzerland
European Health Insurance Card (EHIC)

This covers:

  • state-provided healthcare cover for treatment that becomes necessary whilst temporarily staying in an EU country, Norway, Iceland, Liechtenstein, and Switzerland
  • you if you are a student studying outside of the UK, a UK EHIC will only cover you for state-provided healthcare cover within the EU and your country of study. You will not be able to use your card for treatment in Norway, Iceland, Liechtenstein and Switzerland except where you are studying in one of these countries /li>

When to apply

If you have a UK European Health Insurance Card (EHIC) it will be valid until the expiry date on the card. Once it expires, youâ€™ll need to apply for a UK GHIC to replace it.

Who can apply

During your application you will answer a series of questions about yourself and any spouse, civil partner, durable partner, and children that you add to your application. Based on the information you give us you will be provided with either an UK EHIC or UK GHIC.

Travel insurance and your UK EHIC or UK GHIC

Your EHIC or GHIC card is not a substitute for travel insurance. It may not cover all health costs and never covers repatriation costs. Make sure you have travel insurance as well as an UK EHIC or UK GHIC.
