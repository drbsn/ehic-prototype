Why do I need travel insurance?

Not everything is covered by EHIC/GHIC.

Covered

  • Neccessary medical treatment
  • Prescriptions
  • Hospital stay

Not covered

  • Ambulance journeys
  • Treatment in private hospitals/facilities
  • Additional holiday stay due to illness
  • Specialist travel arrangements

To avoid additional costs make sure you take out travel insurance.
